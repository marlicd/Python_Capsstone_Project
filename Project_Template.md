1. What is the problem you are solving?
> Businesses or your audience care about problems. Make the problem clear and relatable
> For many F1 fans that physically go to watch the raceshave a common problem of forecsting the conditions of the day. 
> The constant change of weather conditions is hard to predict throughout the course of the F1 races.
> My Python code solves that problem by analysing the conditions of the city within those days that the race is happening.



2. What tools did you use?
> I used Wikipedia to webscrape due to it being in HTML format which made it easier to download and scrape
> I used BeautifulSoup from the bs4 module to download and scrape the websites
> I used OpenWeather to get the weather of the city where the race is taking place
